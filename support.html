Frequently Asked Questions

Your Daily Streak increases by one every day you open the app and view an affirmation. To keep your streak going, you must open the app at least once per day. If you miss a day, the streak will reset to zero. You can disable the streak display in the Settings tab if you prefer.

When you are viewing an affirmation on the detail screen, you will see a heart icon in the top-right corner. Tap this heart to save the affirmation to your Favorites. You can view all your saved affirmations by tapping the "Favorites" tab at the bottom of the screen.

Navigate to the "Notifications" tab. Here, you can enable up to three daily reminders: Morning, Midday, and Evening. For each one, you can set a specific time and choose which category of affirmations you'd like to be reminded of. The app must have permission to send notifications, which you can manage in your iPhone's Settings.

We take your privacy very seriously. The app saves all your data (like your name, favorites, and streak progress) directly and only on your device. We do not have servers, and we never collect, view, or share any of your personal information. For more details, please read our full Privacy Policy.

Yes, Daily Affirmations is 100% free. All features, content, and future updates are available to all users at no cost. There are no subscriptions or in-app purchases.
